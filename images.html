Fun Facts

  • Most of the artists lived in The Big Apple and gathered at a tavern in Greenwich Village. Some were venerable European painters who fled war or sought asylum in the city.
  • Many Abstract Expressionists met through a project of painting murals in government buildings. Others were students of German abstraction guru Hans Hoffman.
  • Due to the chaos in Europe and the migration of many artists, New York overtook Paris as the heart of modern art.
  • They looked to Sigmund Freud for theories on dream symbolism, mythology, and primitivism.
  • After the incident, the artists were photographed and featured on Life magazine. Life is art and we seek to paint our own life.
